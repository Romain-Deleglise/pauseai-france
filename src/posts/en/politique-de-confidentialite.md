## Data Collection

Pause IA collects only the data necessary to fulfill its mission:

- **Email address**: when you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us
- **First name and last name**: optionally, when you subscribe to our newsletter

## Use of Data

Your data is used to:

- Send you our newsletter (if you subscribed)
- Respond to your messages
- Manage your membership (if applicable)

## Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your subscription is active, plus a maximum period of 3 years after your last interaction with us.

## Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Rectify your personal data
- Delete your personal data
- Object to the processing of your data
- Port your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: contact@pauseia.fr

## Newsletter Unsubscription

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in each of our emails.

## Cookies

This site uses minimal cookies necessary for its operation. We do not use advertising cookies.

## Contact

For any question regarding your personal data: contact@pauseia.fr
