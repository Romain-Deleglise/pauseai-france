- **[Make a donation](/en/dons) to Pause AI** or buy merchandise from the PauseAI Global online store.

  - [International AI Treaty](https://aitreaty.org/)
  - [Ban Superintelligence](https://www.change.org/p/ban-superintelligence-stop-ai-driven-human-extinction-risk)

- **Follow our channels** on social media and stay informed: [Discord](https://discord.gg/vyXGd7AeGc), [Twitter](https://twitter.com/pause_ia), [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/pause-ia/)
- **Talk** about AI risks on social media.
- **[Send an email to a politician](https://pauseai.info/email-builder)**: Emails are very effective, you might be surprised!
- **Spread the word** by sharing our content with your friends, family, and colleagues.

## Why act now?

AI companies are in an unbridled race to develop increasingly powerful systems. Every week, new capabilities emerge that experts did not anticipate. The window to put adequate guardrails in place is closing.

By acting now, before AI becomes too powerful to regulate effectively, we can prevent catastrophic outcomes and ensure that this technology develops in a way that benefits all of humanity.

## Join the movement

Pause AI France is part of a global movement of citizens, researchers, and experts who believe that the development of the most dangerous AI systems must be paused until adequate governance frameworks are in place.

[Join us today](/en/rejoindre) and be part of the solution.
