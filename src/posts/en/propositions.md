Pause AI advocates for concrete measures to ensure that AI development is safe and beneficial for all of humanity. Here are our main proposals.

## An International AI Treaty

We call for an international treaty on AI that establishes:

- **A moratorium** on the development of AI systems more powerful than current frontiers, until adequate safety standards are developed
- **International verification mechanisms** to ensure compliance with this moratorium
- **An international body** responsible for coordinating AI governance, similar to the IAEA for nuclear energy

## National Regulatory Framework

At the national and European level, we call for:

- **Mandatory safety assessments** before deploying powerful AI systems
- **Strict transparency requirements** on AI system capabilities and training data
- **Democratic accountability mechanisms** for AI systems that affect citizens' lives
- **Protection for AI safety whistleblowers** who raise concerns about dangerous systems

## Corporate Responsibility

We call on AI companies to:

- **Voluntarily pause** the development of systems exceeding current safety thresholds
- **Invest in safety research** at least as much as in capabilities research
- **Publish safety assessments** independently verified before each major new deployment

## Support for Affected Workers

A transition to a world with more powerful AI must be managed fairly:

- **Retraining programs** for workers whose jobs are displaced by AI
- **Adaptation of social protection systems** to respond to job losses caused by automation
- **Fair distribution** of the economic gains generated by AI

## Why These Proposals?

These measures are not about stopping technological progress. They are about ensuring that this progress happens in a way that is safe, equitable, and subject to democratic control. The window to put these guardrails in place is closing — we must act now.
